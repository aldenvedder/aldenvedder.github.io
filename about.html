About Alden Vedder

I specialize in bringing practical solutions to design challenges. Starting from a young age I have enjoyed a hands-on approach to the world around me. I developed hands-on skills fixing appliances, building personal projects, and learning why things work.

This hands-on background was bolstered by a Bachelor of Science in mechanical engineering from Wentworth Institute of Technology, graduating with summa cum laude honors.

I currently am a Medical Device Engineer working on small volume infusion pumps for Diabetes Care.
